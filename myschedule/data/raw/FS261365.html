Forschungskolloquium


Dozent/in Prof. Dr. Bettina Beer
Veranstaltungsart Kolloquium
Code FS261365
Semester Frühjahrssemester 2026
Durchführender Fachbereich Ethnologie
Studienstufe Bachelor Master
Termin/e wöchentlich (Di), ab 17.02.2026, 16:15 - 18:00 Uhr, Inseliquai 10 INE 214
Umfang 2 Semesterwochenstunden
Turnus wöchentlich
Inhalt Join us for a thought-provoking anthropology colloquium where new ideas and work-in-progress will be presented! This is an opportunity for everyone, from students to established scholars, to engage with the latest research and learn about the ongoing dialogues in the field. Our invited speakers explore a diverse range of topics, from the evolution of human societies to the impact of globalisation on local ways of life. Come and be a part of the conversation, ask questions, share your own insights, and connect with others. We welcome all voices and perspectives and look forward to a lively and stimulating exchange of ideas.

Students are invited to participate in the colloquium and can earn two credit points (CP) for regular attendance and active preparation. Additionally, if students form a reading group and engage in collective reading and discussion of selected texts presented in the colloquium, they may be awarded an additional two social CP.
